Site Name

FactLab Global

This name represents a global platform dedicated to sharing interesting and educational facts across different subjects such as science, technology, history, health, and general knowledge. It reflects curiosity, learning, and discovery.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an educational platform that provides engaging and informative facts across multiple categories including science, technology, history, health, and general knowledge. Users will be able to explore daily facts, learn new concepts, and interact with content through quizzes and dynamic features. The platform will also support a future faceless YouTube channel by acting as a content hub.

Scenarios

  • I enjoy learning new things — where can I find quick and interesting facts daily?
  • Can I test my knowledge with quizzes or interactive content on this site?
  • Where can I find short and simple explanations of complex topics like science or technology?

Color Schema

#0B3C5D (Primary Color): Used for header, headings, and footer.

#328CC1 (Secondary Color): Used for navigation, buttons, and highlights.

#F4F4F4 (Background Color): Used for page background.

Typography

Poppins: Used for headings to create a modern and bold look.

Roboto: Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
